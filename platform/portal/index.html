Two journeys

one platform, two audiences
Platform team

Onboard a tenant in one step

A single command provisions an isolated, guard-railed namespace — quota, RBAC, default-deny network, Pod Security, GitOps project, policy enforcement — and issues a namespace-scoped kubeconfig.

Application team

Ship a full-stack app from one commit

Commit manifests; the platform delivers a running app with automatic TLS, mesh mTLS, distributed tracing, metrics, autoscaling and backups — none of it hand-wired.
